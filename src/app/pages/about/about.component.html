Welcome to Nodebucket

Tired of complicated task management software? Then try out Nodebucket!

Nodebucket is an innovative task management software that allows you to easily keep track of your tasks. It simplifies the way you create, track, edit and remove time sensitive material. Nodebucket is a full-stack MEAN (MongoDb, Express, Angular, and Node.js) application that will simplify the way you manage your tasks.

As a user you will be able to login with an employee Id in order to create new tasks. Nodebucket will keep your information secure so only you are able to view it. Nodebucket puts your todo tasks in their own column, while keeping your done tasks in another. By having them separated you'll be able to move items from your todo task list over to your done task list in one smooth step. By having a layout in this manner you won't ever get your todo list and done list confused. Nodebucket also allows you to easily remove items from either list. If you don't want to hang on to an item in your done list after you've completed a task, just simply remove it.
